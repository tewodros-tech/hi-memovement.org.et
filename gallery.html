Empowering Communities in Ethiopia

HI-ME Movement is committed to education, health, and humanitarian support for local communities.

Mekele Donation

HI-ME provides humanitarian aid, child support, and women’s empowerment programs in Mekele, a city of resilience and heritage.

Hawassa Donation

HI-ME supports children’s education, environmental conservation, and youth skill development in Hawassa.

Dire Dawa Donation

HI-ME focuses on youth employment, small business support, and health outreach programs in Dire Dawa. The video shows before and after Donation.

Humera Donation

In Humera, HI-ME supports displaced families, agricultural recovery, and trauma healing programs for war-affected communities.

Axum Donation

HI-ME supports children’s education, environmental conservation, and youth skill development in Axum.

Lalibela Donation

HI-ME supports children’s education, environmental conservation, and youth skill development in Lalibela.
