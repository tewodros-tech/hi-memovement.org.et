Mekele

Mekele, the capital of Tigray region, is a city of resilience and heritage. HI-ME works in Mekele to provide humanitarian aid, child support, and women’s empowerment programs designed to bring sustainable opportunities to the community.

Humera

Humera, located in northwestern Ethiopia, has endured many challenges but remains a community of strength. HI-ME delivers humanitarian assistance, education programs, and health services here.

❮ ❯

Hawassa

Hawassa, a vibrant city in southern Ethiopia, is known for Lake Hawassa and its breathtaking scenery. HI-ME supports schools, healthcare outreach, and livelihoods here.

❮ ❯

Gerji Shoeshine Boy

In Gerji, Addis Ababa, a young shoeshine boy inspired us. HI-ME aims to provide education and vocational training to children like him, helping them build a brighter future.

Virginia

Virginia, USA, is home to a strong diaspora community that partners with HI-ME Movement to support projects in Ethiopia. Events and fundraisers here sustain the mission back home.
