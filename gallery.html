Empowering Communities in Ethiopia

HI-ME Movement is committed to education, health, and humanitarian support for local communities.

Mekele Projects

Mekele, the capital of Tigray region, is a city of resilience and heritage. HI-ME works in Mekele to provide humanitarian aid, child support, and women’s empowerment programs.

❮ ❯

Hawassa Projects

Hawassa, known for its beautiful lake, is a hub of education and culture. HI-ME supports children’s education, environmental conservation, and youth skill development in Hawassa.

❮ ❯

Axum Projects

Axum, a UNESCO World Heritage site, is a city of history and tradition. HI-ME works on cultural preservation, community health programs, and women’s empowerment initiatives.

❮ ❯

Arba Minch Projects

Arba Minch, surrounded by lakes and national parks, is rich in biodiversity. HI-ME focuses on sustainable farming, health clinics, and access to clean water for rural communities.

❮ ❯

Addis Ababa Projects

Addis Ababa, Ethiopia’s capital, is the heart of national development. HI-ME supports street children, vocational training, and community-driven development programs.

❮ ❯

Dire Dawa Projects

Dire Dawa is a diverse city with rich cultural heritage. HI-ME focuses on youth employment, small business support, and health outreach programs in the region.

❮ ❯

Lalibela Projects

Lalibela, famous for its rock-hewn churches, is a spiritual and historical landmark. HI-ME supports tourism-driven local businesses, child sponsorship, and community healthcare initiatives.
