Empowering Communities in Ethiopia

HI-ME Movement is committed to education, health, and humanitarian support for local communities.

Mekele Projects

Mekele, the capital of Tigray region, is a city of resilience and heritage. HI-ME works in Mekele to provide humanitarian aid, child support, and women’s empowerment programs designed to bring sustainable opportunities to the community.

Hawassa Projects

Hawassa, known for its beautiful lake, is a hub of education and culture. HI-ME supports children’s education, environmental conservation, and youth skill development in Hawassa.

Axum Projects

Axum, a UNESCO World Heritage site, is a city of history and tradition. HI-ME works on cultural preservation, community health programs, and women’s empowerment initiatives.
